# Blockchain Protocol Research

Independent research on blockchain protocols with a focus on:
- Structural and systemic risk
- Tokenomics and incentive design
- Governance and upgrade risk
- Real-World Asset (RWA) infrastructure

## Methodology
This research focuses on system-level analysis rather than code auditing.
It evaluates protocol design, incentives, centralization dependencies,
regulatory exposure, and failure modes using publicly available information.
